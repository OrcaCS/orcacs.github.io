ambivalence

welcome. my home is your refuge.

my dream world is a place
in which one can see both the sun and the moon;
in which one can concurrently bask in the polychromatic sky during a sunset and the twinkling stars during the night;
in which one can simultaneously feel the warmth of the sun and the cool breeze of the night.
all while still having the earth beneath one's feet.

i hope this site captures the essence of my dreams.



this is the comforting space i carve out for myself in this vast world.
a place for a break that i hope is serene and refreshing. take as long as you need.

i welcome you just as i welcome myself. make yourself at home.


it's a little basic in here right now, though basic isn't inherently bad. this page will simply develop along with me on my journey of life and HTML learning.

may we cross paths somewhere, sometime in a promising and limitless world.
