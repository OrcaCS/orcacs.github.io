the lake

you find yourself at a peaceful lake surrounded by calm forest.
an inviting place for quiet introspection.
a resting spot to pause and appreciate all one has in their life.


To see a world in a grain of sand
And a heaven in a wild flower,
Hold infinity in the palm of your hand
And eternity in an hour.

—William Blake, "Auguries of Innocence"


I think the real miracle is not to walk either on water or in thin air, but to walk on earth. Every day we are engaged in a miracle which we don't even recognize: a blue sky, white clouds, green leaves, the black, curious eyes of a child—our own two eyes. All is a miracle.

—Thich Nhat Hanh







the colourful leaves dance on the branches.


the trees always seem to be magically colorful: as though they are stuck in the phase of autumn, as though time has stopped here. this place will always be here, exactly as you came and exactly as you left it.

sit and stay a while. refind the peace in your heart.
